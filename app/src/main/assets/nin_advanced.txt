Extra Tips

Notes are unfiled by default, but you can file them to different folders easily. Set shortcuts to those folders for quick filing.

In the list view, you can select notes by tapping their leftmost side.

Tap and hold an item to see its context menu.

That's about it, this app is pretty simple once you get the hang of it. Most of the functionalities are discoverable.
